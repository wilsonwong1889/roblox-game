# Week 2 Blueprint

Archived Week 2 baseline.

The Week 2 build established the first economy loop and the claim/respawn foundation. It remains useful as a reference, but the active validation baseline has now moved to Week 3 docs.

Core Week 2 facts that remain relevant:

- one shared lobby spawn
- two claimable tycoon plots
- one tycoon max per player
- claim does not teleport immediately
- future respawns route to the owned tycoon
- starter wallet cash and collector stored cash
- purchase buttons for the first upgrades
- visible money parts travelling to the collector
- manual collect from the money pad
- reusable tycoon objects driven from config
