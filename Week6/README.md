# Week 6

Week 6 expands the game into an 8-tycoon ring layout.

## Scope

- 8 claimable tycoons
- 2 on the left
- 2 on the right
- 2 on the front
- 2 on the back
- every tycoon front faces inward toward the center
- central hub decorations
- corner decorations
- shared lobby spawn in the center
- working owner-claim flow
- working shared door access after owner opens the door
- starter manual dropper plus first-floor and second-floor progression
- fortified perimeter walls with health and paid rebuilds
- roaming NPC soldier patrols outside the tycoon walls
- start-menu daily spin with weighted cash rewards
- bottom-right code redeem panel for bonus cash

## Validation Focus

- map layout and spacing
- door open/close behavior for owners and non-owners
- claim flow across all 8 tycoons
- starter manual dropper path
- collector and auto-collector behavior
- second-floor access
- wall damage, destruction, and paid repairs
- NPC soldier spawning and roaming around the perimeter
- daily spin GUI and weighted reward payout
- code redemption flow and wallet rewards
